📅 GRE Prep: 2-Month Study Plan

Follow this structured plan to maximize your score.

✅ Structure: Full-length Mock Tests | Mini Quizzes | Targeted Learning | Progress Tracking
🟢 Subscriptions Required: 2-Month Gregmat | 1-Month Magoosh

📌 Goal: Understand current level, build strong fundamentals.
  • Mock Tests:
    • 📝 Magoosh
    • 📝 Gregmat
    • 📝 Magoosh
  • Daily Practice:
    • 1 Mini Quiz (Quant & Verbal)
    • 20 Vocabulary Words (Gregmat Vocab Mountain / Magoosh App)

  • Concepts to Focus On: Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry Basics, RC Strategies

📌 Goal: Improve accuracy in weak areas.
  • Mock Tests:

    • 📝 ETS
    • 📝 Manhattan

    • 2 Mini Quizzes (Targeting Weak Areas)

  • Concepts to Focus On: Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning

📌 Goal: Increase speed and accuracy.
  • Mock Tests:
    • 📝 Manhattan
    • 📝 Manhattan
    • 📝 Manhattan
  • Daily Practice: 2 Mini Quizzes, Vocab Usage, RC Skimming Strategies
  • Concepts to Focus On: Probability, Mental Math, Trap Answer Choices

📌 Goal: Simulate real exam conditions.
  • Mock Tests:
    • 📝 ETS (Final Official Test)
    • 📝 Full-Length Gregmat
    • 📝 ETS (Final Confidence Booster)
  • Daily Practice: Final Review, Mixed Drills, Vocabulary Revision
  • Concepts to Focus On: Time Management, Test Stamina
